# Code of Conduct for Halal Market Online

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors to Halal Market Online. We expect all contributors to follow this code of conduct.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive of others.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be open to constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Trolling or disruptive behavior.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement
If you witness or experience any unacceptable behavior, please report it to us at `conduct@halalmarket.online`. We will investigate all reports and take appropriate action.
